My expertise involved creating premium, hyperlocal digital experiences for Nike consumers around the world. I worked closely with digital engineering, product, and site experience operations teams to document and drive internationalization requirements, ensuring that Nike's digital presence was tailored to the specific needs of each market. In consultation with UX teams, we utilized pseudo-localization tools to enhance international usability, creating a seamless and intuitive experience for users across the globe.

As a core team member, I played a pivotal role in procuring and onboarding new Translation Management Software (TMS) at an enterprise level. This involved collaborating closely with various stakeholders to ensure that the new software could meet Nike's operational needs and seamlessly integrate into the existing digital infrastructure, further enhancing the internationalization capabilities of the organization.
