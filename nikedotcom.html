As a seasoned professional in internationalization, my expertise involved creating premium, hyperlocal digital experiences for Nike consumers around the world. I worked closely with digital engineering, product, and site experience operations teams to document and drive internationalization requirements, ensuring that Nike's digital presence was tailored to the specific needs of each market. In consultation with UX teams, I also utilized pseudo-localization tools to enhance international usability, creating a seamless and intuitive experience for users across the globe.

In addition to my technical expertise, I also had experience in documenting and prioritizing product specifications, including goals, use cases, and requirements. This involved conducting user interviews with individuals from diverse cultural backgrounds, using their insights to design features that better served their needs. As part of my role, I also oversaw the Nike.com localized experience quality review program, ensuring that every aspect of the user experience met the highest standards.

As a core team member, I played a pivotal role in procuring and onboarding new Translation Management Software (TMS) at an enterprise level. This involved collaborating closely with various stakeholders to ensure that the new software was seamlessly integrated into Nike's existing digital infrastructure, further enhancing the internationalization capabilities of the organization.
