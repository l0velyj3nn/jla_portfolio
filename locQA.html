Nike.com Localization Quality Program

Nike.com LQA Lead

April 2016 to January 2019

It's more than translation! As the leader of the localization user experience quality program for nike.com, I collaborated with teams to address content issues, set comprehensive quality standards, track and resolve bugs, and deliver a premium user experience for diverse markets.

I developed a set of annotated templates of the entire site to aid in routing defects to the appropriate team. Paired with an effective triage process, we were able to expediciously resolve issues.
